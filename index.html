ABOUT

In March 2014,Red Devils Sim Racing was established, to test the limits of the competitive nature that pushes forward every sim racer..... every form of racecar driver actually....ever sportsperson we suppose....you get the idea. We believe it is important to develop the ability to find a strong mix of competitive nature between teammates and other drivers, collaboration and proffesionalism. Once this can be mastered by all involved then upward progression will be neverending. Strong support along with mastering how to focus and concentrate competive nature on the task at hand instead of aimlessly trying to progress could only be for the better of everyone involved we thought, so we decided to put the theory into practice.

By the closing stages of 2014, we had experienced superb development from the team members, leading to breaking lap records and successful attempts at obtaining pro licences! This opened the opportunity to recruit many rising stars from a range of different series from across the globe to help ensure that they could reach their personal potentials by learning from our key values and beliefs for progression in sim racing, who can now teach others. This has led to wider recognition throughout sim racing along with multiple championship victories across a variety of series along with very strong showings at a variety of Iracing special events and prestigious league series.

The history looks bright, but the future looks even brighter! Happy Racing!
